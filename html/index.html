What is OpenBloo

Open Bloo is an experience network that connects people to places, activities and events in real-time to help build amazing memories and moments the define our lives.

Features

Recieve places, activities and events personalized to your interests using the Experience Feed. Scroll high-quality photos to quickly navigate and select expereinces that peek your interests. Filter your results to narrow down results, or bookmark and organize experiences for later.

OpenBloo was made for friends, family and co-workers in mind. Create a party with the people of your choice and start finding experiences that relate to everyone's interests.Plan vacations and special days with "Invites" which sync to your mobile device calendar to ensure everyone is on the same page.

The postcard for the 21st century has arrived. High-quality photos which capture the essence of an expereince are displayed on your expereince feed. Simply flip the card to the back and recieve information on the expereince in one seamlesss gesture. No more navigating through excessive pages to find basic information.
